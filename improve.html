The How

This is basically taking each event and for every single team computing the percentile score their first match fell into and the last, then it averages the scores over the event. The premise is that in general later matches should be higher scoring as teams refine their machines and understand how to play the game.
A green arrow denotes a shift to later matches trending towards higher percentile scores. A red the opposite. If the premise that teams get better as events go on holds, it should be mostly green.
